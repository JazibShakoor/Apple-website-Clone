    Description

    amana Trading & Investment app provides its users access to 1000+ financial instruments on its platform. With this online trading app, you will be able to trade easily in many different assets with ZERO commission. Use your smartphone to trade, train, and analyze the markets. You can buy and sell everything from:


    • Regional stocks: ADX, DFM


    • Global stocks: Apple, Tesla, Google, Microsoft


    • Natural Gas, WTI Crude Oil & Brent Crude Oil


    • Indices: S&P500, Dow Jones


    • ETFs & more...


    amana is an All-in-one trading platform, complete with fractional trading.



    FEATURES YOU’LL LOVE:


    Enjoy exceptional design and a user-friendly experience with personalized watchlists, news, notifications, and more.



    WHAT MAKES US DIFFERENT:


    1. Trading fit for everyone


    We empower everyone with access to global markets, regardless of gender, wealth, or expertise. We offer you a minimum deposit policy and enable you to trade whatever products excite you, whether you’re a professional or a beginner.


    2. Tuition-free learning


    When you join the amana app, you become part of a diverse community of investors. And with our dedicated Learn section, you can learn, grow, and own your know-how, on the go.


    3. Fast & easy accessibility


    We connect people with markets worldwide—including regional markets—quickly and seamlessly. With our easy-to-use trading app and jargon-free lingo, everyone has an equal opportunity to own their finances.


    4. No minimum balance


    There’s no minimum balance to be maintained in your account. Invest as little as you want, whenever you want. You can add more funds later when you’re more experienced.


    5. Broad range of products


    Our app offers you a rich variety of financial products for all kinds of portfolios and investing goals. Discover a full range of products, from stocks, ETFs, and more.


    6. Pricing & Transparency


    We keep it real with a clear breakdown of costs and fees. We also offer you zero-commission trading on thousands of products and even fractional trading


    And when it comes to regional products, what you pay is what we pay. That means that we don’t make any profit on spreads or commissions. And as our community grows, so does our ability to lower prices for everyone


    Download the app now and unlock the power to invest in your future



    TRADE THE WORLD:


    Global stocks


    Enjoy zero-commission trading on US shares and stock derivatives of companies worldwide—from the US to the EU, MENA, and beyond.


    Regional stocks


    Power up your portfolio with the local brands you know and love. Invest in the leading MENA stocks and benefit from the growth around you.


    And the best part? We don’t make a profit from regional stock trading, add a markup or charge a commission. We simply pass along our costs from our clearer and the exchanges so that everyone can access regional stocks at the best price


    Digital Currencies


    Invest in the Digital Currencies market with 0% commission and 100% support. Trading & Investing in Digital Markets has never been this easy.



    WHAT ABOUT SUPPORT?


    Our expert team is here to support you 24 hours a day, 5 days a week. Simply drop us a message at support@amana.app and we’ll be right with you


    So, what are you waiting for? Download the app today
